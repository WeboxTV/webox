FAQ
How do I post new movies and tv-shows if they don't already exist?
Click on Request tab and then click on Submit new stream link.
Can I submit links for shows and movies if I find them?
Yes you can. Find the movie or episode of relevance and click on Submit New Stream Link. Enter the information and click submit.

What if a link of movie - TV SHOW is not working?
Go to Request tab and click on link not working,we will remove that link with new link ASAP.

Link submission rules:

1. Links must be playable on submission
2. Links must be for the correct movie/episode
3. All links must have English audio if available.
4. Audio must be in sync with the video
5. No watermarks in the link
Any link that does not meet these rules will be removed and/or reported.
